What are “intelligent processes”

Think about the tasks your team repeats every day: training a new agent, reviewing call quality, creating scripts for a new campaign, compiling performance data. Today someone does them by hand — or they simply don’t get done. With the AI Setup, those tasks become processes that execute in seconds — your team just gives the instruction and the system does the rest.

The 5 processes we will build for Unified Systems

These are the specific intelligent processes designed for your operation, based on our discovery conversation.

Process 01
Call Simulator — Medicare & Insurance
Before

New agents go live on the dialer without formal practice. They burn real leads while learning. Training depends on whoever is available on-site.

After

Agents practice realistic Medicare, Ancillary, and Final Expense calls with an AI that simulates real customers — with objections, questions, and compliance scenarios. They get scored after every session.

Process 02
Agent Onboarding Assistant
Before

Onboarding depends on a person being present to explain everything: dialer, protocols, compliance, disposition codes. Inconsistent quality across locations.

After

A guided, interactive onboarding system walks new agents through every campaign protocol, dialer usage, and compliance rule — with quizzes to verify understanding. Works the same in Pakistan, Utah, or Colombia.

Process 03
QA Scorecard — Call Quality Analyzer
Before

QA listens to 20 calls a day manually. With 300-400 agents, most calls go unreviewed. Compliance issues slip through.

After

AI analyzes 100+ call transcripts per day against your compliance scorecard: correct introduction, mandatory questions asked, buffer time met, proper disposition. Flags red flags and suggests coaching per agent.

Process 04
Campaign Script Generator
Before

Scripts live in people’s heads. Opening a new campaign or language means manually writing and translating everything. Takes days.

After

The system generates complete scripts per campaign and language: opening, qualification questions, objection handling, transfer protocol. New campaign in Spanish? Ready in 2 minutes.

Process 05
Daily Performance Digest
Before

You have to dig through the dialer’s clunky reports to understand what happened yesterday. Or depend on someone telling you.

After

Every morning you get a 2-minute executive summary: top and bottom agents, campaigns vs. target, coaching alerts, anomalies. Control your operation remotely from your phone.

Estimated impact: ~43 hours per week recovered across training, QA, script creation, and daily reporting. Your team focuses on closing deals and scaling campaigns — not on manual supervision.
