CONTEXT
What we understood about Unified Systems
Unified Systems runs a 300-400 person call center operation across Pakistan and Utah, handling Medicare, ACA, Final Expense, and Ancillary insurance campaigns. They use VICIdial as their dialer. Now they want to scale into the Spanish-speaking market with 80-200 new agents — but their core challenge is operating remotely without being physically present.
“I personally cannot look day to day operations. I would need somebody who I can work with and who can handle all these things.” — Abdul Raheel, Co-Founder & CEO
CURRENT SITUATION
What we found
No formal agent training
New agents go live on the dialer without structured practice. They learn by burning real leads. Training quality depends on whoever happens to be available.
QA doesn’t scale
With 300-400 agents, manual call review covers less than 5% of calls. Compliance issues and underperformance slip through undetected.
Scripts live in people’s heads
Each campaign has different scripts, but they’re not documented. Opening new campaigns or languages means starting from scratch every time.
No remote visibility
Operations data is locked in the dialer’s clunky interface. There’s no way to know what happened yesterday without someone manually telling you.

Agent Onboarding Assistant

1
Supervisor activates onboarding for a new agent and assigns their campaign
2
System guides step by step: campaign protocol, dialer usage, disposition codes, compliance rules
3
Mini-quiz per module + completion checklist generated automatically
Estimated impact: ~8 hours/week — eliminates dependency on in-person trainer
03
Quality Assurance
SKILL 3
QA Scorecard
With 300-400 agents, manual call review covers less than 5% of calls. Compliance issues slip through. For a remote call center, this is even more critical.
1
QA manager loads call transcripts (single or batch)
2
AI evaluates against campaign scorecard: introduction, mandatory questions, compliance, buffer time, disposition
3
Generates scorecard per agent + red flags + suggested coaching actions
Estimated impact: ~15 hours/week — QA goes from 20 calls/day to 100+ analyzed automatically
04
Content
SKILL 4
Campaign Script Generator
Each campaign has a different script. Opening in Spanish means manually translating and culturally adapting everything. Scripts are not documented anywhere.
1
Operations manager selects campaign and language
2
System generates complete script: opening, qualification questions, objection handling, transfer protocol
3
Output includes scenario variants ready to load into the dialer or print
Estimated impact: ~5 hours/week — new campaigns/languages ready in minutes instead of days
05
Reporting
SKILL 5
Daily Performance Digest
With operations in Pakistan and expansion to Colombia, there’s no way to know what happened today without digging through the dialer’s clunky reports or depending on someone to tell you.
1
System takes daily data from the dialer (manually or automated)
2
Analyzes against KPI targets: top/bottom agents, campaign performance, anomalies
3
Generates 2-minute executive summary: coaching alerts, red flags, action items
Estimated impact: ~5 hours/week — remote control of operations in 2 minutes from your phone
