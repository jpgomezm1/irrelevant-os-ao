Product 3: Centralized CRM

03
Lead & Client Management System

Capabilities:

  • Automatic lead capture from website (forms and chat assistant)
  • Direct integration with intelligent assistant—every conversation becomes a lead
  • Unified lead capture from multiple channels (social media, phone, email, web)
  • Follow-up tracking and status management for each lead (new, contacted, scheduled, converted)
  • Complete interaction history per client
  • Segmentation by service of interest (injectables, laser, wellness, skincare, etc.)
  • Dashboard with key metrics: total leads, conversion rate, services in demand
  • Automatic alerts and reminders for follow-ups

Centralization principle: A scattered system loses information, duplicates effort, and wastes opportunities. A centralized CRM ensures no lead is lost and every interaction is tracked.

Product 4: Appointment Scheduling Assistant

04
Automated Scheduling System

Features:

  • Conversational bot for appointment scheduling via chat
  • Direct connection with CRM—every appointment is automatically logged
  • Email integration for automatic confirmations
  • Automatic confirmation emails after booking
