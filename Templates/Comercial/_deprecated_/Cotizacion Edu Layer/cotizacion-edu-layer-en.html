What is Think AI

A 3-hour workshop where your team goes from not understanding AI to thinking and solving problems with it. This is not a motivational talk or a theoretical lecture — it’s a hands-on session where every participant finishes using AI tools applied to their actual work.

Your team doesn’t need to become AI experts. They need to learn to think with AI to solve the problems they already face every day.

Workshop Curriculum

The workshop is structured in two progressive blocks: first the team understands what’s behind AI and how it changes the way you approach problems, then moves to hands-on practice with real tools.

Block 1 — 45 minutes
Think Different
Content
  • What AI really is — No technical jargon. How it works, what it can and can’t do.
  • How it changes the way you see problems — From “we need more people” to “can a system do this?”
  • Opportunity map — Where AI creates real value in a company like {{EMPRESA}}.
  • Real cases — Concrete examples of companies in the region already operating differently.
Outcome

The team understands what AI is and starts seeing their daily problems through a new lens.

Block 2 — 2 hours 15 minutes
Do Different
Core tool: Claude Suite (Anthropic)
  • Claude — Intelligent conversation to analyze, summarize, draft, and solve complex problems.
  • Claude Code — Advanced task execution: automate processes, connect tools, generate reports.
  • Claude Cowork — Real-time AI collaboration within the team’s workflow.
Skills developed
  • Effective prompting — How to communicate with AI for useful results. Structures, techniques, and common mistakes.
  • Analysis with AI — Turn data, documents, and scattered information into clear decisions in minutes.
  • Content and document generation — Proposals, reports, communications, meeting notes — all in seconds.
  • Exercises with real {{EMPRESA}} cases — Each person solves a real problem from their day-to-day using AI.
  • Workflow integration — How to incorporate AI into daily routines so it becomes habit, not experiment.
Outcome

Each participant leaves with real tools they can use starting the next day, with solved exercises and the confidence to do it on their own.

Important: Content is adapted to each company’s specifics and the tools they already use. Before the workshop, we hold a brief context session to customize exercises and examples to the team’s industry and operation. The foundation is prompting + the Claude suite, complemented with {{EMPRESA}}’s specific platforms.
