What are “intelligent processes”

Think about the tasks your team repeats every week: summarizing a meeting, writing a client follow-up, building a report, creating a proposal. Today someone does them by hand. With the AI Setup, those tasks become processes that execute in seconds — your team just gives the instruction and the system does the rest.

Examples of processes we deliver

These are real examples. The specific 5 processes for {{EMPRESA}} are defined during the diagnosis, based on what generates the most impact in your operation.

Example
Meeting → Notes + automatic tasks
Before

Someone takes notes during the meeting, cleans them up, sends them by email. Takes 30-45 min. Sometimes nobody does it.

After

The meeting is recorded, the system generates notes with decisions, assigned tasks, and deadlines. Published automatically. Takes 0 minutes from your team.

Example
Client data → Proposal ready to send
Before

The sales rep opens an old template, copies data, adjusts pricing, checks for errors, asks for approval. Takes 2-3 days.

After

The system takes client information and generates a professional proposal with pricing, scope, and terms. Ready in 30 seconds.

Example
Sales call → Personalized follow-up
Before

The salesperson finishes the call and has to write the follow-up manually. Sometimes they forget or send it 3 days later.

After

The system listens to the call, identifies key points, and generates a personalized follow-up message for WhatsApp or email. Ready in 2 minutes.

Important: These are just examples. The 5 processes we implement for {{EMPRESA}} are chosen during the diagnosis, prioritizing those that generate the greatest impact in time recovered and execution quality. They are 100% adapted to your operation, your team, and your tools.
